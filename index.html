Our Story

Born from a
family kitchen

Daisy Coffee grew out of something real. Our family has been behind Daisy Maison for years — handmaking personalised gifts from our Lancashire studio, earning the trust of customers across the UK and beyond.

"I've always believed your morning sets everything. I started blending my own collagen coffee at home and I couldn't stop. That was the beginning of all this."

When the woman who built Daisy Maison started blending her own collagen coffee every morning — experimenting with roasts, flavours, different origins — we knew it was time to share it. Daisy Coffee is her passion, bottled. Shared with the same care we've put into every gift we've ever made.
