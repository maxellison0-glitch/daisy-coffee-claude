About this blend

We spent a long time getting this one right. The collagen coffee blend started as something Shelley made at home every morning — trying different roasts, different ratios, different flavours — until it felt exactly right. What you get in the bag is the result of that. A smooth, balanced coffee that doesn't taste like it has anything added, because good marine collagen should be tasteless. Just a really good cup of coffee that quietly does a bit more for you.

What's inside

  • 10g marine collagen peptides per serving
  • Carefully sourced arabica coffee beans
  • No artificial flavours or fillers
  • No added sugar
  • Suitable for cafetiere, filter and pour over

How to brew

  • Use one heaped tablespoon per cup
  • Water temperature around 93°C — just off the boil
  • Brew for 4 minutes in a cafetiere, 3 in a filter
  • Works beautifully with oat milk if you prefer it white
  • Best enjoyed as your first cup of the day

Storage

Keep in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, reseal the bag and use within 4 weeks for the best flavour. The marine collagen is stable at room temperature and doesn't need refrigerating.
